1. Feature Suggestions ￼

Core Banking Features:
 • User registration & authentication (JWT or OAuth2)
 • Wallet creation & management
 • Deposit, withdraw, transfer between users
 • Transaction history with filtering (date, type, etc.)
 • Balance inquiry
 • Account locking/freezing

Advanced Features:
 • Scheduled/recurring payments
 • Multi-currency support
 • Notifications (email, SMS, in-app)
 • Admin dashboard (view users, transactions, etc.)
 • Fraud detection (basic rules)
